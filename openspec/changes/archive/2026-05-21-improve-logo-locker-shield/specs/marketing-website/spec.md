## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Brand Logo Identity
The system SHALL use a distinct tu-locker brand logo based on a storage-building + padlock concept instead of relying only on the `TL` placeholder.

#### Scenario: Visitor sees the site header
- **WHEN** a visitor views the header
- **THEN** the logo communicates storage and security through a storage-building + padlock visual mark together with the `tu-locker` wordmark

#### Scenario: Visitor sees small logo contexts
- **WHEN** the logo is displayed in compact contexts such as favicon-sized usage or tight navigation space
- **THEN** the mark remains recognizable and does not depend on small unreadable text

### Requirement: Logo Consistency Across Site
The system SHALL apply the new logo consistently in primary brand locations including the header, footer, and available site icon metadata.

#### Scenario: Visitor reaches brand locations
- **WHEN** a visitor sees the header, footer, or browser/site icon surfaces
- **THEN** those locations use the same storage-building + padlock visual identity or an appropriate simplified version of it

### Requirement: Logo Visual Fit
The system SHALL keep the logo aligned with the existing professional blue, gray, and white visual language of the marketing website.

#### Scenario: Logo appears on light and dark backgrounds
- **WHEN** the logo appears on the white header or dark footer
- **THEN** it maintains sufficient contrast, legibility, and visual harmony with the surrounding design
