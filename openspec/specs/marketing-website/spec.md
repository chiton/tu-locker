## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Complete Spanish Landing Page
The system SHALL provide a complete Spanish marketing website for tu-locker that communicates self-storage and guardamuebles services for personal and commercial customers at `tu-locker.com.ar`.

#### Scenario: Visitor reviews service overview
- **WHEN** a visitor opens the home page
- **THEN** the page presents a clear hero message, service summary, primary calls to action, and Spanish content for tu-locker

#### Scenario: Visitor understands the business purpose
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the initial content
- **THEN** the page explains that tu-locker rents secure spaces for storing personal objects, furniture, moving items, and commercial belongings

### Requirement: Home Hero And Calls To Action
The system SHALL include a home hero section with a clear security-focused message, a brief service summary, and calls to action for viewing plans and requesting a quote.

#### Scenario: Visitor lands on the site
- **WHEN** a visitor opens the page
- **THEN** the visitor sees a prominent message such as "Espacio seguro para lo que mas valoras" and visible actions such as "Ver planes" or "Solicitar presupuesto"

### Requirement: Required Business Sections
The system SHALL include sections for Inicio, Sobre Nosotros, Ubicacion, Seguridad, Tamanos de Storage, Planes y Formas de Pago, Como acceder, and Contacto.

#### Scenario: Visitor navigates through required sections
- **WHEN** a visitor uses the top navigation
- **THEN** the visitor can access every required business section from the same page

### Requirement: About Company Content
The system SHALL include a Sobre Nosotros section with a brief company history, mission, vision, and values of security, trust, and accessibility.

#### Scenario: Visitor evaluates the company
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the Sobre Nosotros section
- **THEN** the visitor sees who tu-locker is, what the company aims to provide, and which values guide the service

### Requirement: Storage Size Options
The system SHALL present three storage size options: pequeno, mediano, and grande, including approximate dimensions, usage examples, and estimated prices.

#### Scenario: Visitor compares storage sizes
- **WHEN** a visitor reaches the storage sizes section
- **THEN** the visitor sees the three options with enough information to identify the most suitable option

#### Scenario: Visitor identifies usage examples
- **WHEN** a visitor compares the size cards
- **THEN** the pequeno option mentions boxes and personal objects, the mediano option mentions furniture from an apartment, and the grande option mentions full moves or commercial use

### Requirement: Plans And Payment Methods
The system SHALL present monthly, quarterly with discount, and annual with larger discount plans, and list accepted payment methods: card, bank transfer, and cash.

#### Scenario: Visitor reviews payment options
- **WHEN** a visitor reaches the plans and payment section
- **THEN** the visitor sees plan durations, discount positioning, and available payment methods

### Requirement: Trust And Security Content
The system SHALL explain the security features including cameras 24/7, controlled access, individual alarms, lighting, and monitoring.

#### Scenario: Visitor evaluates safety
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the security section
- **THEN** the visitor understands why stored belongings are protected at tu-locker

#### Scenario: Visitor checks specific security measures
- **WHEN** a visitor scans the security cards or list
- **THEN** the page explicitly mentions cameras 24/7, controlled access, individual alarms, lighting, and monitoring

### Requirement: Location And Access Information
The system SHALL show the address Montevideo 1450, Bahia Blanca, Buenos Aires, Argentina, include an embedded map, and explain access information.

#### Scenario: Visitor looks for facility location
- **WHEN** a visitor reaches the location section
- **THEN** the visitor sees the address, a map, and practical access details

### Requirement: Rental Access Process
The system SHALL explain the rental process step by step, access hours, and secure access through key or code.

#### Scenario: Visitor wants to know how access works
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the Como acceder section
- **THEN** the visitor understands the steps to request a quote, choose a storage size, sign up, and access the unit securely

### Requirement: Contact Conversion Paths
The system SHALL provide contact paths through a contact form with name, email, and message fields, phone, email, normal WhatsApp call-to-action links, and a floating WhatsApp button for +54 291 4239860.

#### Scenario: Visitor wants to request a quote
- **WHEN** a visitor clicks a WhatsApp call to action
- **THEN** the visitor is taken to a WhatsApp conversation with tu-locker

#### Scenario: Visitor uses contact details
- **WHEN** a visitor reaches the contact section
- **THEN** the visitor sees phone and WhatsApp `+54 291 4239860`, email `contacto@tu-locker.com.ar`, and a visible quick WhatsApp button

### Requirement: Responsive Professional Design
The system SHALL use a modern, clean, professional, responsive design using blue, gray, and white tones associated with security and trust, with icons, clearly differentiated sections, illustrative storage imagery, and gentle animations.

#### Scenario: Visitor uses a mobile device
- **WHEN** the site is viewed on a mobile viewport
- **THEN** all content remains readable, navigation remains usable, and calls to action remain accessible

#### Scenario: Visitor uses tablet or desktop
- **WHEN** the site is viewed on tablet or desktop viewports
- **THEN** sections use appropriate spacing, card layouts, imagery, and calls to action without horizontal overflow

### Requirement: Basic SEO And Deploy Readiness
The system SHALL include basic SEO metadata, clear page title, meta description, semantic sections, organized project files, a top navigation menu, and a footer with contact data, address, and social links or placeholders.

#### Scenario: Search engine reads metadata
- **WHEN** the home page is crawled
- **THEN** the page exposes relevant title and description metadata for tu-locker.com.ar

#### Scenario: Visitor reaches the footer
- **WHEN** a visitor scrolls to the footer
- **THEN** the visitor sees contact data, address, domain reference, and social links or placeholders

### Requirement: Optional Trust Enhancements
The system SHALL include testimonials and frequently asked questions about storage when they can be implemented without external dependencies.

#### Scenario: Visitor has common doubts
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the FAQ section
- **THEN** the visitor sees answers about contracts, access, payments, security, and suitable storage sizes
